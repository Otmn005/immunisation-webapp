Our Mission Statement

Understanding our purpose and the people we serve

Project Purpose

Vaccination has been a pivotal method in eradicating diseases like smallpox and achieving global herd immunity for others, such as polio. The World Health Organization (WHO) gathers data from member countries to assess vaccination rates, incident rates, and mortality rates for preventable diseases.

Our Immunisation Dashboard allows various stakeholders to explore detailed and unbiased vaccination data. We present this information in an informative, respectful, and unbiased manner, catering to a diverse range of users who are seeking to become more informed on this critical public health topic.

By using our website, users can become well-informed about global immunisation efforts and their impacts, enabling data-driven decisions to improve public health outcomes worldwide.
