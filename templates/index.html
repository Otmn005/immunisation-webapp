Welcome to the Global Immunization Dashboard

Stay informed about vaccination coverage and disease prevention worldwide.

What You'll Find Here:

  • Vaccination data coverage from 2000 to 2024
  • Total doses administered and cases tracked each year
  • Interactive data on preventable diseases and vaccine coverage
  • Insights by country, region, and disease type

Key Vaccination Facts

  • Timeframe Covered: Vaccination data available from 2000 to 2024.
  • Total Number of Vaccines Administered: Millions of doses have been recorded across all countries and years.
  • Total Number of Cases of Preventable Diseases: Cases for diseases such as measles, rubella, diphtheria, and more are tracked annually.
  • List of Infectious Diseases Tracked: DTP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis), Measles (MCV1, MCV2), Rubella (RCV1), Hepatitis B, Polio, and more.
